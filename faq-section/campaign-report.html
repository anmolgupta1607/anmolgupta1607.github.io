• How can I track performance of my campaigns?

      You can track campaign performance for a selected date range in Overview tab. You can search for a product to view performance of the specific product.

      • Impressions: The number of times this product was shown to the end user as an ad.
      • Clicks: The number of times this product ad was clicked.
      • CPC or Cost per click: The price you need to pay per click. Default price is Rs 2.
      • Spends: The amount spent on serving the particular product ad.
      • Conversion: It denotes number of products sold through advertising the product.

    • Can I view performance of a specific campaign and a specific product for a specific date range?

      Yes, you need to apply appropriate filters to view the performance data.
