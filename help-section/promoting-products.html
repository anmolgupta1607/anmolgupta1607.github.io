Here are few simple steps to get started with Snapdeal Ads.


1. Login to your seller panel.
2. Ensure that you have enough funds in your ad account that will help you promote your products.
3. Click on “Add Products”.
4. Select any product that you would like to promote.



Note that you will only be able to add products which are in stock and your campaigns will run only if your Overall rating in all fulfillment modes is 4.5 and above.

Click on “Start” when done. Your products are now being advertised instantly.
