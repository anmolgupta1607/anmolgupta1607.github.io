You can start ads by adding funds using your CoD outstandings due with Snapdeal. You simply need to click on Add Funds, enter the amount within the Ads limit and click on Add. Minimum amount per transaction should be Rs 1000.

Ads limit is 30% of CoD outstandings. This is capped to this amount to ensure that it is recoverable from your next payment.



Transaction History

Here you can view a daily summary of all your debit and credit transactions.
Any funds that a seller adds towards ads are shown as credits and or any funds used towards displaying ads are shown as debits here.
Debits are done on a daily basis, so if all total spends in a day were about Rs 5000, you will see a Rs 5000 debit in the Transaction history page.
