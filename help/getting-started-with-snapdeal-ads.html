Here are few simple steps to get started with Snapdeal Ads.

1. Login to your seller panel.

2. Click on Advertising in the left menu.

3. Ensure that you have enough funds in your ad account that will help you promote your products.

Note that you will only be able to advertise products which are in stock and your campaigns will run only if your Overall rating in all fulfillment modes is 4.5 and above.
