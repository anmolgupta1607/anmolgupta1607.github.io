How do I check the performance of my campaign?

Basic performance details of the campaigns such as - total impressions and clicks - are available in the campaign list on Snapdeal Ads platform

To analyse the impressions and clicks for a campaign on a specific date, use 'Reports' tab available in left hand menu on Snapdeal Ads platform. 1. Click on 'Create Banner Campaign'.

You can select from various options -

1. Specific campaign or all campaigns.

3. Metric to analyse - Impressions or Clicks

'Reports' screen shows campaign trend in graphical format. You can also download the selected details using the 'Download' link

Can I check the performance of the campaign on a particular day?

Yes. Log on to Snapdeal Ads and use 'Reports' tab available in the left hand menu. You can select from various options -

1. Specific campaign or all campaigns

2. Date range

3. Metric to analyse - Impressions or Clicks

'Reports' screen shows campaign trend in graphical format. You can also download the selected details using the 'Download' link
