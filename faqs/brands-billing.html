What are the different billing options available?

Currently, Cost-per-Thousand Impressions (CPM) billing mode is available.

How is the budget spending on the campaigns managed?

After you submit the campaign, Snapdeal Ads team reviews it and updates the pricing as agreed in the contract. Ads platform ensures that campaigns are run as per the schedule and other targeting criteria specified while setting up campaign in the system.
