In our effort to help Sellers use self service to meet their business needs, Snapdeal Ads announces the launch of Ads help center - a fully self service help desk for Snapdeal Ads customers.

Through this help center, you will be able to access help documentation, videos, FAQs and more.

Help center is accessible within your ads panel.

You can also access the help centre home page from ads.snapdeal.com web site.

The help centre provides you help docs, videos, guided tours and more to help you quickly learn the ads panel.

You can quickly learn to use the ads panel by taking a tour (available from within your panel).

Still have open questions?

If your questions are still unanswered, you can write to us at adsales@snapdeal.com and we will revert back to your questions within 1 business day.
