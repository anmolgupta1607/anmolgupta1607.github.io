Introduction to Snapdeal Advertising

The Snapdeal Advertising platform caters to the need of the Sellers and Brand to reach out to the customers through Ad delivery. It provides customized ad delivery to user there by pursuing them to buy the product they are interested in. It adopts a data driven method to deliver ads there by The Snapdeal Advertising aims to increase the ROI for Sellers through Ad delivery.

Performance Terms Summary
Ad Request: An ad request occurs when a user's requests an ad from us. There is one request per ad slot on a given user interface.
Impression: An impression is generated every time an ad is delivered and the user is exposed to it.
Click: The act of clicking on an ad that has been served.
Conversion: An Conversion is generated every time when purchase happens through ad delivery.
CPM (Cost Per Mille): CPM ads, also known as CPI (Cost Per Impression), are paying per impression. Such campaigns are formed on the basis of what it pays to show the ad to one thousand viewers.
CPC (Cost Per Click): The price an advertiser pays for each click on their ad CTR (Click Through Rate): The percentage of overall impressions that resulted in a click on an advertisement.
eCPM (effective Cost Per Mille): eCPM is a performance indicator. It indicates how much revenue is generated for the advertiser, on average, per 1000 impressions. In short it is earnings divided by the number of impressions, multiplied by 1000.
